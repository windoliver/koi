## Vision

### 10x Check
The 10x version: Koi becomes the "Salesforce for AI agents" in financial services. Every hedge fund, family office, and boutique shop runs Koi. Vertical skill packs auto-configure for each firm type. Nexus connects to Bloomberg, email, CRM, compliance databases, and market data feeds. The community registry has firm-specific skills shared across the industry. ACE means every deployment gets smarter over time. Compliance officers trust it because session replay + governance presets handle SEC/FINRA requirements out of the box.
